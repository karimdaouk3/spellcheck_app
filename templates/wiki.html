FSR Coach Wiki

A quick guide to using FSR Coach effectively.

← Back to App Submit Feedback

1. Overview

FSR Coach helps you craft clear, thorough problem statements and FSR Daily Notes. The left side is where you type; the right side shows guidance and rewrite questions.

  • Use the microphone to dictate, or type directly.
  • Click “Submit for Review” to get a score and detailed feedback.
  • Answer any rewrite questions to improve your text and get a suggested rewrite.

2. Temperature Bar (Vague → Thorough)

The header of each editor shows a temperature bar indicating thoroughness. Aim to move the purple indicator to the right over time.

  • Vague (left) → Thorough (right)
  • Target is near the middle/right; keep iterating after reviews.

3. Running a Review

  • Type or paste your content, then click “Submit for Review”.
  • Open the “How Your Score Was Calculated” panel to see each criterion.
  • For items marked “Needs Improvement”, click to reveal rationale and tips.

4. Rewrite Questions

When criteria need improvement, you’ll see targeted questions. Your answers persist while switching between the two editors. They reset only when a new review is run.

  • Provide short, specific answers.
  • Click “Rewrite” to generate an improved draft.

5. History

The History section keeps recent versions. Click any item to restore it and view its score and feedback. Quality labels (Needs Improvement, Good, Excellent) appear below each entry.

6. Tips

  • Use the copy button to quickly copy your text (you’ll see a checkmark on success).
  • Use the mic for quick dictation; long pauses can reduce transcription quality.
  • Submit feedback from the top-right button to help improve FSR Coach.
